Hi. I'm a person on the internet.

I made this website because I had things to say and I needed somewhere to say them that wasn't owned by a venture-backed company with a 10-year plan to monetize my attention.

I write about things I find interesting. I build things occasionally. I think most software is too heavy, most websites are too slow, and most "solutions" are looking for a problem.

If you've landed here from somewhere, welcome. If you've landed here from a search engine, hello, you're one of the few people who still uses URLs.

---

This site is built with [Astro](https://astro.build). The CSS weighs less than the README. No trackers, no analytics, no JavaScript unless absolutely unavoidable.

If something is broken, I probably know. If it's not broken, I probably left it intentionally.
